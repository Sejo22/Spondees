About us

We are seasoned booksellers with owner's collective 50+ years of experience in the industry.

Operating within the realm of ecommerce, we have established robust systems that can be effectively leveraged for library sales. Our presence spans across Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

We believe API's can improve the Acquisitions process for printed books. Rather than relying on printed PDF catalogs or emails, we offer API solutions that seamlessly integrate with library systems for effortless ordering. Whether your library requires books sourced from overseas or elusive titles from ecommerce websites, we are here to assist. Additionally, if you have specific printed books on your wish list, we can help procure them.

Our commitment extends beyond providing API solutions. We are eager to collaborate with Library Management Systems (LMS) to bring the benefits of sourcing imported books directly to library customers.

At Spondees, our data is continuously updated in real-time as we expand our network of vendors and sourcing avenues. We look forward to supporting your library's needs and facilitating access to a diverse range of books from around the globe.

Extensive data

Our database is updated in real-time to reflect new releases, prices, and availability.

Orders can be conveniently placed directly from your library management system, making us your go-to local vendor for imported books. We continue to expand our reach and catalogue, ensuring that exchange rates, international delivery charges, and sales restrictions are updated in real-time. We embrace all types of books, including those with Unicode characters.

Please note that we do not offer cataloguing metadata services like MARC or Onix. Instead, consider us as a bookstore with extensive reach and coverage.

Verified Supply Chain We source our books directly from publishers or their authorized distributors, ensuring that only 100% legitimate books are procured.
